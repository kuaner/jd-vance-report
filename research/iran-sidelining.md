## 1. The Atlantic: "The Humiliation of J. D. Vance"

**Source:** The Atlantic, March 5, 2026
**Author:** Idrees Kahloon
**URL:** https://www.theatlantic.com/ideas/2026/03/vance-declining-relevance-iran/686234/

### 核心论点

> "Trump's war with Iran shows that, within the administration, the vice president's opinions matter less and less."

### 关键事实

- Vance 在 2024 竞选期间明确承诺美国不会对伊朗开战："America doesn't have to constantly police every region of the world"
- 2024.09 与 Shawn Ryan 播客：伊朗以色列战争是"引发第三次世界大战最可能和最危险的场景"
- 打击开始后，Vance 的 X 账号"异常安静"
- Vance 不在 Mar-a-Lago 与 Trump 一起指挥，白宫释放的照片显示他在白宫主持"次要会议"，身旁是一罐 Diet Mountain Dew 和表情阴沉的 Tulsi Gabbard

### Vance 的政策主张与现实的差距

**外交政策：**
- Vance 是 MAGA 联盟中的孤立主义一端
- 有连贯的反对干预理论：美国不能同时在多条战线作战，不应将稀缺弹药消耗在地区冲突上（如俄乌战争），因为还要应对崛起中的超级大国中国
- 现实：导弹拦截器正以惊人速度消耗在一场中东 discretionary war 中

**经济政策：**
- 曾想将儿童税收抵免扩大到每个孩子 $5,000
- 想通过扩大工会化、拆分大科技公司、加速反垄断执法、提高关税来保护美国工人
- 推崇"匈牙利式"家庭政策
- 现实：The One Big Beautiful Bill Act 仅将儿童税收抵免提高到 $2,200/孩子。"除了关税——那本来也是 Trump 几十年的执念——几乎看不出任何 Vanceism"

**AI 政策：**
- 2025 巴黎演讲反对"过度监管 AI"，承诺"保护美国 AI 和芯片技术免受窃取和滥用"
- 现实：2025.12 授权向中国出口先进芯片以换取 25% 的收益分成
- 2026.03 试图打压 Anthropic（因与五角大楼的谈判失败）

### Signal 聊天泄露

- Vance 在私人 Signal 聊天中表达了对打击胡塞武装的反对意见
- 被否决后"迅速顺从"（acquiesced）
- Elbridge Colby（五角大楼最高政策官员，Vance 盟友）曾在 2025 夏季决定暂停对乌克兰的武器运输

### The Atlantic 的结论

> "Vance came to public attention as a resounding critic of Trump who could nonetheless explain his appeal among heartland Americans to coastal elites. [...] Reinvention is obviously possible for a man who has reinvented himself before. But if he seeks the Republican nomination for president in 2028, he may find himself bound to an unpopular series of policies that the 2024 version of Vance would oppose."

## 2. NY Mag: "Is J.D. Vance Feeling Left Out of Trump's Big New War?"

**Source:** New York Magazine / Intelligencer, March 12, 2026
**Author:** Ed Kilgore
**URL:** https://nymag.com/intelligencer/article/what-does-trump-iran-war-mean-for-jd-vance.html

### 核心论点

> "While Trump's Iran war may violate every MAGA principal allegedly held by J.D. Vance, it's worth remembering that he is nothing without Trump, and his presidential ambitions mean grinning and bearing it all."

### 关键事实

- SOTU 演讲前三天，Trump 宣布 Vance 将领导"反欺诈战争"（War on Fraud）
- 但当实际炸弹落下时，Vance "very much hors de combat"（完全被排除在军事行动之外）
- Vance 的影响方式主要是"在社交媒体上保护 Trump 的 MAGA 侧翼"
- 不需要 Vance 向 MAGA 基本盘推销战争——他们已经因 Trump 的声明而 gung-ho
- 不需要 VP 欢呼——Trump 的所有媒体出口都在高强度鼓吹战争

### Vance 的权力基础分析

> "His base of power isn't Republican voters, but Republican elites. He's spent his entire adult life currying favor with more powerful patrons — progressing from Amy Chua, to David Frum, to Peter Thiel, to Tucker Carlson, and finally to Donald Trump's children."

> "Instead of making himself popular with voters, Vance chose to work the inside game. He targeted people with enough juice to give him the prizes he wanted."

### 对 2028 的影响

- "任何 Trump 副总统都离被抛弃只有一步之遥"
- Vance 在共和党政治中的根基远不如前任 Pence（后者同时受到保守派福音派和国防鹰派的喜爱）
- 必须永远活在 2016 年"America's Hitler" 的阴影下
- 如果伊朗战争变得类似于他曾经谴责的"forever wars"，他只能"grin and bear it"

### 其他 NY Mag 引用的 Vance 相关分析

- **3/11/2026**：GOP "反欺诈"运动实际上是大规模安全网削减
- **3/10/2026**：Trump 政府难以控制油价飙升
- **3/10/2026**：伊朗战争给了民主党中期选举的新制胜议题
